Welcome to the Computer Science Department

The Computer Science Department, a proud component of the School of Physical and Mathematical Sciences at the University of Ghana, is dedicated to fostering excellence in computer science education and research. Our department is characterized by a vibrant and diverse community of students and scholars.

Academic Programs

We offer a range of academic programs to cater to different interests and career goals:

  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSc. IT)
  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Sciences (BSc. Computer Sciences)
  • Master of Philosophy (MPhil) in Computer Science
  • Master of Science (MSc) in Computer Science
  • Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Computer Science

Enrollment and Training

Applications are currently open for our intensive eight-week training program. Choose between virtual or on-campus learning experiences tailored to your preferences. Our world-class faculty and state-of-the-art facilities ensure a rich and immersive educational journey.

Why Choose Us?

Joining the University of Ghana Computer Science Department provides you with:

  • Expert faculty with extensive industry and research experience
  • Cutting-edge laboratories and technology resources
  • Opportunities for collaboration with leading professionals and researchers
  • A supportive and inclusive learning environment
